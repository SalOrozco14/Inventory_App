Izakaya Sushi

About

Welcome to Izakaya Sushi inventory controller! In this app you can keep track of both Inhouse and Outsourced ingredients. As well as customized Sushi rolls with the ingredients found in the inventory. This app is perfect for sushi chefs, restaurant managers or back kitchen staff to keep track of the amount and types of sushi rolls available with the current inventory.


Functionality is simple and easy. Whenever youâ€™re adding a new ingredient, click on the corresponding Add button. If the ingredient is Inhouse for example: freshly grown carrots or homemade soy sauce. You would add it under the Inhouse category. Outsource ingredients for example: specialty fish, exotic ingredients. Would fall under this category. Fill out the basic form and submit to add the ingredient to the inventory.


Adding sushi rolls to the list is simple. Click the add button and fill out the form with the basics. Next add the ingredients needed for the Sushi roll and done! You can update the item by simply clicking the update button, or delete the item if it is no longer available.
